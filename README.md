Retail Sales Performance & Profitability Dashboard

Executive Summary
This project focuses on commercial data analytics and business intelligence (BI). By processing and modeling a large-scale retail dataset, this project delivers interactive executive dashboards designed to track Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), analyze regional sales trends, and identify the most lucrative product categories to drive strategic corporate decision-making.

Technical Stack
Business Intelligence: Power BI 
Data Manipulation: Advanced MS Excel (Power Query, Data Modeling)
Core Concepts: Data Visualization, Executive Reporting, Time-Series Analysis

Methodology
1.Data Ingestion & Transformation:
Imported complex relational datasets containing multi-year records of corporate Orders, Customer Returns, and Regional Sales Representatives.
Established data relationships and cleaned formatting to ensure accurate metric aggregation across multiple tables.
2.KPI Engineering:
Designed high-level executive scorecards to instantly communicate critical business metrics, including Total Sales Volume, Gross Profit, and Average Order Value.
3.Trend & Time-Series Analysis:
Engineered interactive line charts to track month-over-month sales performance, allowing stakeholders to identify seasonal purchasing trends and revenue spikes.
4.Categorical & Regional Segmentation:
Developed dynamic visualizations (bar and pie charts) to break down sales volume by Geographic Region (West, East, Central, South).
Isolated profitability metrics by Product Category (Technology, Office Supplies, Furniture) to identify the highest and lowest margin sectors.
